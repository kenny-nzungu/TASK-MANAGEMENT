About Task Manager

Our Mission

To simplify project management for educational institutions by connecting students, lecturers, and project managers through intuitive digital tools.

Who We Are

Task Manager was founded in 2022 by a team of educators and software developers who recognized the need for better project management tools in educational settings. Our platform bridges the gap between traditional academic workflows and modern project management methodologies.

We're dedicated to improving collaboration between students, lecturers, and project managers by providing a centralized platform for task assignment, tracking, and communication.
